2024 ICC Men's T20 World Cup

Groups and Teams

The tournament will feature 20 teams divided into four groups:
Group A: USA, India, Pakistan, Canada, Ireland
Group B: England, Australia, Namibia, Scotland, Oman
Group C: West Indies, New Zealand, Afghanistan, Uganda, Papua New Guinea
Group D: South Africa, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, Netherlands, Nepal【9†source】

Schedule Highlights

The tournament opens with the USA playing Canada in Dallas on June 1, 2024. - Key fixtures include India vs. Pakistan on June 9 in New York and Australia vs. England on June 8 in Barbados【9†source】.

Prize Money

The total prize pool is $11.25 million, with the winners receiving $2.45 million. Each team will earn additional prize money for each match they win【7†source】.

Security and Logistics

CARICOM nations are collaborating to ensure security and smooth operations, with measures such as mutual visa recognition and a single domestic space for the duration of the tournament【10†source】.
